Family Suite

Going away with the family? If so, then you are probably seeking the perfect accommodation in Shimla. If this sounds like you, then one of our family rooms could be perfect for you. With a family room you get two rooms that are connected together for your exclusive use.

This gives you the chance to enjoy all the space you need and to stay with your family, but still have them close enough to be safe and sound. With the whole family staying together, your whole stay can be made as relaxing and fun as possible.

As rooms in Shimla go, you’ll get a luxurious, spacious room packed with modern amenities – what more could you need?
